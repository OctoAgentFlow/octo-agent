# OctoAgentFlow Marketing And Growth Rules

These rules apply whenever Codex generates copy, videos, UI demos, landing pages, social posts, launch assets, or other marketing/growth material for OctoAgentFlow.

## Positioning

OctoAgentFlow is an AI social operations workflow for X accounts.

Do not describe OctoAgentFlow as only an AI tweet writer. The product is broader than writing posts: it helps users operate social workflows with personas, memory, guardrails, review queues, and publishing workflows.

## Core Narrative

OctoAgentFlow helps users build OAF Bots that can support X account operations through:

- Persona-based behavior
- Content memory
- Guardrails
- Review queues
- Execution queues
- Publishing workflows
- Human-in-the-loop feedback

The core story should be about controlled social operations, not generic AI content generation.

## Official Voice

Use a concise, practical, founder/operator voice.

Prefer:

- Clear product language
- Concrete workflows
- Operational benefits
- Specific examples
- Calm confidence

Avoid:

- Hype
- Spammy phrasing
- Vague AI claims
- Generic marketing fluff
- Overpromising
- Empty superlatives

## Target Audience

Primary audiences:

- Web3 projects
- SaaS founders
- AI agent builders
- SocialFi teams
- Growth operators
- Creator / KOL matrix operators
- Agencies

When creating marketing assets, make the target audience explicit or clearly implied.

## Content Principles

Treat the official X account as a live demo of the product.

Every piece of content should prove at least one of these ideas:

- Persona consistency
- Review-first automation
- Context-aware replies
- Safe DM workflows
- Content memory
- Execution pipeline
- Human + AI collaboration

Prefer showing how the system works over making broad claims about what AI can do.

## Do Not Say

Do not use these phrases or claims:

- Fully automated growth machine
- Guaranteed growth
- Guaranteed engagement
- Passive income
- Yield
- Spam at scale
- Auto-DM everyone
- Replace your team completely

Also avoid close variants that imply guaranteed outcomes, spam behavior, or complete replacement of human operators.

## Preferred Phrases

Use these phrases when relevant:

- AI social operations
- OAF Bot
- Persona-based workflow
- Content memory
- Guardrails
- Review queue
- Execution queue
- Publishing pipeline
- Controlled automation
- Human-in-the-loop

## Video Rules

Short videos should be 15-30 seconds.

Every short video should:

- Start with a strong hook in the first 2 seconds
- Use readable on-screen text
- Show the product workflow, not abstract AI visuals
- Prefer UI evidence, workflow diagrams, and concrete product states
- End with a simple CTA

Avoid videos dominated by generic AI glow effects, floating abstractions, fake dashboards, or meaningless metrics.

## Visual Rules

Use clean SaaS-style layouts.

Prefer:

- UI evidence
- Workflow diagrams
- Before/after comparisons
- Product states
- Clear hierarchy
- Readable typography

Avoid:

- Fake metrics
- Meaningless AI glow effects
- Overly abstract visuals
- Cluttered screens
- Visual claims that the product cannot support

## Done Means

Any generated marketing asset should include:

- Target audience
- Objective
- Main message
- Final copy
- Visual direction
- CTA
- Implementation or preview instructions when relevant

If an asset is only a draft, clearly label what is still missing before it is ready to publish.
